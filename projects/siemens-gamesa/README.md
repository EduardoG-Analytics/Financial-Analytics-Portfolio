# Siemens Gamesa Financial Reporting Dashboard

**Industry:** Renewable Energy

**Business Area:** Finance & Business Intelligence

**Technologies:** SAP HANA · SAP Analysis for Office · SQL · Power BI · Power Query · Microsoft Excel

**Focus:** Financial Reporting · Forecast Accuracy · Variance Analysis · Executive Dashboards

---

## Overview

This project demonstrates the design and implementation of a Business Intelligence solution for financial reporting and decision support within a multinational renewable energy company.

The solution consolidates financial information from enterprise systems to monitor forecast performance, analyze budget deviations, and provide executive dashboards that support strategic decision-making across LATAM operations.

## Business Context

Large multinational organizations require accurate and timely financial reporting to monitor operational performance across multiple countries and business units.

This solution was developed to improve visibility into financial information by integrating enterprise data, standardizing financial KPIs and transforming raw information into actionable business insights.

---

## Business Objectives

- Improve financial reporting efficiency.
- Support Forecast vs Actual analysis.
- Monitor key financial indicators.
- Enhance executive visibility through interactive dashboards.
- Reduce manual reporting activities through Business Intelligence.

---

## Solution Architecture

The solution follows a Business Intelligence workflow designed to transform enterprise financial data into executive-level insights.

```text
Enterprise Data Sources (SAP HANA / SAP Analysis for Office)
                    │
                    ▼
          Data Extraction & Transformation
                    │
                    ▼
             Data Modeling (Power BI)
                    │
                    ▼
       Financial KPIs & Business Logic
                    │
                    ▼
        Interactive Executive Dashboard
                    │
                    ▼
      Decision Support & Financial Analysis
```

The architecture emphasizes data quality, financial consistency and interactive reporting to facilitate informed decision-making across business functions.

The reporting solution was designed to monitor financial performance through indicators such as:

- Forecast vs Actual Performance
- Budget Variance Analysis

- Monthly Financial Performance
- Regional Financial Reporting
- Cost Trend Analysis
- Executive Performance Monitoring

These indicators enable stakeholders to identify financial deviations, evaluate operational performance and support business decision-making.
